💡 Study Strategy Tips

🌅 Two-Slot System

Study new material in the morning when cognitive clarity is highest. Use evening for spaced repetition review before sleep.

🔁 Spaced Repetition

Review words just as you're about to forget them. Use Anki to automate this — it optimizes your time by focusing on tricky words.

🗣️ Shadowing

Repeat Japanese audio immediately after hearing it. This builds phonetic reflexes needed for the listening section.

🎧 Passive Immersion

Listen to Japanese podcasts/music even while doing other tasks. Helps your brain recognize speech patterns over time.

😴 Sleep & Rest

The brain consolidates language during REM sleep. Maintain a regular sleep schedule — it's a linguistic necessity.

📚 Limit Resources

Stick to 2-3 core materials. Avoid "resource paralysis" — 3 hours focused beats 6 hours distracted.
